## Strategic Insights
- **Rails**: Best for startups or SaaS platforms needing fast iteration and marketplace-style features.  
- **Laravel**: Strong choice for **sales and inventory systems** tied to e-commerce, thanks to its PHP ecosystem and integrations.  
- **Django**: Ideal for **enterprise-grade inventory management**, especially where **data integrity, analytics, and security** are critical.
